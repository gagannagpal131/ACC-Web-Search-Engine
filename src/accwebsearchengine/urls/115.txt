The Office of Health & Safety: 1. Manages the university's overall health and safety program with the goal of preventing injuries and illness. 2. Manages the University of Windsor's Chemical Control Centre 3. Develops and implements policies and procedures to meet the requirements, duties, and standards set by the Occupational Health & Safety Act and its applicable regulations and other applicable legislation. The office also facilitates return to work programs and accommodation according to the Workplace Safety & Insurance Board's Operating Policies for a successful early and safe return to work.
