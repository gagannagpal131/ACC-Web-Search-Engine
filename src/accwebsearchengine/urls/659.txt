We are currently seeking qualified instructors to assist in the facilitation of our Additional Qualification (AQ), Additional Basic Qualification (ABQ) and Principal's Qualification (PQP) courses. Teaching AQ/ABQ/PQP courses is an excellent way for you to share your knowledge and expertise with your colleagues in this collaborative learning environment. We have garnered an excellent reputation for offering over 80 AQ courses facilitated by instructors that have a passion for the course content and the ability to deliver the course material in an engaging and inspiring manner. Our AQ courses are fully online which is perfect for busy, working professionals. As well, AQ courses are delivered via our Blackboard learning management system. The exception would be our Health and Physical Education and Principal's Qualification Program courses which are partly face to face. Please make sure to read the document titled CallforInstructorsWinter2020.pdf found at the bottom of this web page. Interested applicants will submit applications directly to the Faculty of Education by completing an online application. Please contact aq@uwindsor.ca should you require assistance. We thank all applicants in advance for their interest in being an AQ instructor for Faculty of Education, however, only successful applicants will be contacted. Successful candidates will be contacted towards the middle of November, 2019. The deadline for receipt of application is Sunday, November 3, 2019.
