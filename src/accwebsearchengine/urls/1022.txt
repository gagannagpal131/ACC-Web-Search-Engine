The UWin Plan gives you $500 for purchases at the Campus Bookstore and $195 for use at any food location on campus, including our franchise partners. When you purchase the UWin Bookstore & Food Plan, you will be rewarded with a 10% BONUS ($50), added to your Bookstore Plan. If you run out of UWin Bookstore Dollars, you can add money in increments of $100; you will continue to be rewarded with a 10% BONUS added to your Bookstore Plan. Your UWin Bookstore & Food Plan does not expire while you are a student at the university; any money not spent by the end of the year will automatically carry over to the next year. In addition to the convenience and savings you get with the UWin Bookstore & Food Plan, you will be eligible for "Plan Member" special offers and sales throughout the year, with discounts on clothing and general merchandise at the Campus Bookstore.
