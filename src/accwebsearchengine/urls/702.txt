When you support the Odette School of Business, whatever the amount, your gift makes an impact. Making a contribution to the Odette School of Business helps to provide students with the tools for a great education, one that helps them develop their talents and establish their place in the world. Supporting the Odette School of Business is a smart investment in students and their campus environment. You may choose to support the following needs or specify another designation of your choice: student scholarships student recognition events undergraduate and graduate student case competitions faculty chairs facilities expansion Your gift will help bright young minds grow and thrive. Please remember, all gifts are tax-deductible and you will receive a receipt. To learn more about how to make a contribution, contact Odette Philanthropy and Engagement Officer, Kelly Gosselin at (519) 253-3000 ext. 3137 or Kelly.Gosselin@uwindsor.ca Or you might consider investing in Odette by completing the secure online giving form below. Please remember to designate your donation to the Odette School of Business in the appropriate place on the form.
