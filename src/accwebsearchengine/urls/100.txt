In order to transfer a column containing the values for your students' final grades from Blackboard's Grade Centre, you need to be the Instructor of Record on that course. Locate the Transfer Final Grades tool in your Control Panel -> Course Tools -> Transfer Final Grades. View a short video on this tool. More on using this tool in the Transfer Final Grades from Blackboard to UWinsite Student article.
