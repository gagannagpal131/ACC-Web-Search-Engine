The support shown to the Faculty of Nursing through donations to the annual giving program and to specific projects, such as the Simulation Labs in the Clinical Learning Centre, is greatly appreciated. You can make your donation and indicate "Clinical Learning Facilities Expansion" on the online donation form. A receipt for income tax purposes will be issued.
