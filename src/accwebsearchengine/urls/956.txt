The Ontario Registered Music Teachers' Association (ORMTA) is a not-for-profit provincial organization managed by a volunteer council and comprised of more than 1300 Registered Music Teachers and related members across Ontario. The aim of the ORMTA is to encourage and provide the highest caliber of music education possible and to promote exceptional standards of music in each community. www.ormta.org The Community Concert Series provides an opportunity for Windsor and Essex County music students to perform solo and chamber pieces, with access to a high-quality grand piano. As valued members of the musical community, we invite your private students to participate. Pianists, instrumentalists, and vocalists are all welcome as soloists or in small ensembles, with or without accompaniment.
