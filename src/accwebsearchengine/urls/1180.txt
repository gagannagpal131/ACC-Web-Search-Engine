Some students may get their tuition fully covered by OSAP. For the loan portion, there are also programs to help you repay your student loan once you’re done school. Both the Ontario and Canadian governments provide this money. Visit OSAP Visit UWindsor Windsor/Essex County is a beautiful region situated in the heart of the Great Lakes.
