Explore UWindsor's Campus Visit The Joyce Entrepreneurship Centre The Joyce Entrepreneurship Centre is home to the Entrepreneurship Practice and Innovation Centre also known as the EPICentre, Career Development and Experiential Learning. From makerspace to incubation space, the EPICentre is the place where like-minded students meet, industry professionals network, and mentors offer advice. Career Development & Experiential Learning's advisors help students build bridges between the classroom and working world by offering programs and resources designed to assist with career planning, job search strategies, resume writing, interview preparation, and more. Visit CDEL Entrepreneurship, Practice & Innovation Centre (EPICentre) Got a great idea? Want to build your entrepreneurial skills? From makerspace to incubation space, EPICentre is the place where you can meet like-minded students, network with industry professionals and connect with mentors. Stop by today to find out how you can Be EPIC! Visit EPICentre Ignite: Workstudy Earn money while building your skills through UWindsor’s Ignite program. It funds 400 part-time student jobs on campus each year. Visit Ignite VIP: Community Service Learning Help others and build your resume by getting career- related experience at a non-profit or publicly funded agency.
