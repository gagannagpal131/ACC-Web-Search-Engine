Our faculty are active in research and are internationally recognized. Our faculty to student ratio (1:16) is the best in Ontario! We are national leaders in undergrad research that will have a lasting influence as you prepare for your career. Have a passion for cancer research or the infinite world of Artificial Intelligence? We’re the place for you! We offer high-impact learning experiences from exploring the volcanoes of Iceland to the jungles of Costa Rica. Your UWindsor science degree will combine the right elements of classroom instruction, research opportunities, internship or co-op possibilities, and hands-on learning for an extraordinary career. Explore Programs Explore UWindsor's Campus Visit the Essex Hall of Research (CoRe) The Faculty of Science’s research and innovation facility, the Essex Centre of Research - also known as CORe - is dedicated to research, innovation, and industry collaboration. It boasts three floors of open-concept lab space and meeting rooms devoted to research in advanced materials including nano-technology and biometrics, translational health with an emphasis on cancer research, and medical physics including imaging and diagnostic technologies. It's a place where faculty and students come together. Take a virtual tour Book a campus tour Science Shadow Days This half-day experience includes being hosted by a current UWindsor Science undergraduate student, who will take you for a short campus tour, lunch (sponsored by the Dean of Science) in the CAW Student Centre, and to sit in on a science undergraduate class. You may also choose to attend an optional full campus tour offered by the Office of Student Recruitment after your shadowing experience.
