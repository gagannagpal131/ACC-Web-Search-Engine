Student Third Year and Graduation Recitals Third year and graduating Music students will perform solo recitals in the Armouries Performance Hall or at a variety of venues around Windsor. Community Concert series at SoCA Armouries. Sundays at 2 pm these free recitals feature performances by young musicians in the studios of ORMTA teachers and other music teachers in our community.
