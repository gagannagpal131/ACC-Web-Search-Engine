The facility is equipped with modern workstations, wireless internet, private meeting rooms, workshop room, kitchenette and ample collaborative and comfortable spaces. EPIC Innovation houses students and entrepreneurs participating in the EPIC Discovery, RBC EPIC Founders, Libro EPIC Social Enterprise and EPIC Incubation programs as well as our regional innovation Centre WEtech Alliance. We also hold a wide variety of workshops, competitions and networking events delivered by industry professionals to help students and community members on their entrepreneurial journey. EPIC Innovation houses students and entrepreneurs participating in our Discovery, Founders and Incubation programs. Industry partners who collaborate with the University of Windsor can also access the facility through the Industry Incubation Program and EPIC Soft Landing Program. EPICentre hosts most of its events, workshops and competitions in this location. Click here to Meet Our Startups. Location: 2nd Floor, Joyce Entrepreneurship Centre, 2455 Wyandotte St. West, Windsor, ON N9B 0C1 EPIC Industrial Hub The Windsor Mold Group EPIC Makers’ Base is a makerspace well equipped with 3D printers, CO2 laser cutter/engraver, sewing machine and other hand tools for members to create prototypes, to learn new skills or simply to tinker. Visit EPIC Makers’ Base for more information on the program and how to join the free membership. In addition to being a makerspace, the Windsor Mold Group EPIC Makers’ Base houses startups participating in the EPIC Incubation Program who need industrial space for product/prototype development. We also hold the Make It & Take It Workshop series in this facility.
